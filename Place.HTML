Siddheshwar Temple

One of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located near Siddheshwar Lake.

Pandharpur

A sacred town 70 km away, famous for Vitthal Rukmini Temple. Known for Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi.

Akkalkot

Located 40 km away, famous for Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj temple.

Solapur Fort

Historic Bhuikot Fort built during Bahmani Sultanate period.
